Lesson 1 — What is Congklak?

👋 Welcome to Congklak Academy, Player! Long ago in Indonesia, kids played Congklak after school to train their brains 🧠 It looks simple… but it’s secretly a strategy battle 😎 Your mission: Collect MORE stones in your house 🏠 than your opponent. Ready to become a Congklak master?

Lesson 2 — Starting the Game

🎮 Let’s start the game! Every small hole begins with 7 stones. Pick ONE hole on your side and drop stones one by one clockwise. It feels easy at first… but every move changes the future 👀

Lesson 3 — Extra Turn Rule

🔥 SECRET POWER UNLOCKED 🔥 If your last stone lands in your HOUSE 🏠 You get ANOTHER TURN 😱 Yes… you can play again immediately. Smart players chain turns together to dominate the board.

Lesson 4 — Capture Rule

😎 Ninja move time… If your last stone lands in an EMPTY hole on your side, you steal ALL stones from the opposite hole! Sneaky? Yes. Powerful? VERY. This move can flip the game instantly.

Lesson 5 — Winning the Game

🏆 The FINAL battle The game ends when one side runs out of stones. Time to count treasure! Whoever has the MOST stones in their house wins. Victory belongs to the smartest strategist 🧠✨
